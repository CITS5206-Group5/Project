With a warm and compassionate approach, we offer a safe, non-judgmental space where you can explore your feelings, gain insight, and develop effective strategies to improve your overall well-being.

At Inner Works Counselling, we believe everyone has the power to create positive change in their lives. Our experienced counsellors are committed to supporting you on your journey, offering guidance and encouragement as you work toward your goals. Whether you are seeking support for anxiety, depression, post separation and divorce, grief and loss or other life challenges, we are here to help you find your path to healing.

Our services are tailored to your unique needs and preferences. We offer individual therapy as well as specialised counselling for specific issues such as divorce, trauma and life transitions. Our flexible scheduling options include in-person counselling and online counselling, making it easy to access the support you need when you need it.

We believe building a strong therapeutic relationship is key to successful outcomes. That is why we prioritise open communication, trust and mutual respect in our work with clients. Our collaborative approach empowers you to take an active role in your healing journey, fostering personal growth and resilience.
